Dr. Animesh Naskar has been associated with MAKAUT (formally known as WBUT) as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Food Science and Technology since September 2021. Prior joining to the MAKAUT he served as an Assistant Professor at HMCE for 2.5 years and also had short term industrial exposure. Dr. Naskar completed his graduation and post-graduation in Microbiology from the University of Calcutta. He was awarded RGNF for pursuing Doctoral research and earned Ph.D from Jadavpur University in 2018 in the domain of Applied Microbiology. He has also qualified GATE in Life Science in the year 2010.
During doctoral research, he had broad-spectrum exposure and his research interests span over several topics like microbiology, bioremediation, wastewater treatment, biopolymers, protein chemistry, enzymology, environmental biotechnology and nanotechnology. Till date, he has published numerous research papers in reputed peer-reviewed journals (cumulative impact factor: 32.909; over 114 citations, h-index-5) in his credit and is the co-author of other few book chapters.
He has participated in many (more than 20) National and International Seminars/Conferences/Workshops/FDPs presenting his research works and was awarded a couple of times for his presentations. One of his doctoral research works has been awarded as an “outstanding appreciable paper” at National Seminar on Recent Advances in Biotechnology in 2015 organized by Biotech Research Society, India. Moreover, his recent work has been highlighted in the “Nature-Asia blog” and appeared in “The Hindu” English newspaper on 26 September 2016.
Dr. Naskar has the ownership of Editorial Board Member of two International peer-reviewed journals (“Science Journal of Chemistry” and “IJARTET”) and also the reviewer of esteemed international journals like Chemosphere, Environment and pollution, Environmental Engineering Research, Separation Science and Technology, Desalination and Water Treatment, among others. His research interest includes Environmental Biotechnology, Enzyme & Microbial Technology, Fermentation Technology, Low-cost food product development, Functional Foods, Nanotechnology, Artificial intelligence approach.
