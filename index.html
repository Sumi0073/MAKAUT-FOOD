VISION:

The Department of Food Science and Technology, MAKAUT envisions being a primary resource, for food science knowledge, research, innovation, and talent in creating competent professionals, and in developing the advanced entrepreneurs to ensure the food and nutritional security and sustainable food systems for the coming decennia.


MISSION:

The mission of the Department of Food Science and Technology, MAKAUT is to contribute to the global community through the provision of world-class education by:

  • Educating students from a diverse background in the fundamental skills, knowledge and practice of Food Technology in order to prepare them for a position in global industries and continue for advanced degrees in Food Science and Technology or related disciplines.
  • Offering high-quality education and training to the students preparing for careers in Food Science and Technology in the food industry, academia, or government.
  • Providing basic and applied research and professional services to streamline and optimize operations which contribute to the ultimate benefit of the food industry and consumers as well as to the enhancement of the quality of life.



MESSAGE FROM HOD:


The Department of Food Science at Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT, W.B), Haringhata, West Bengal was founded in 2019 with MAKAUT's academic wisdom. Postgraduate (M.Sc) and Undergraduate (B.Sc) programs in Food Science and Technology and Food Science and Nutrition are offered by the Department of Food Science and their curricula are recurrently revised to meet the changing needs of the industry and significant stakeholders. In addition, the Department offers a research centre that leads to a Ph.D. program.


The remarkable and qualified faculties with their academic excellence and real-world experience are devoted and committed to teach the curriculum. Students are encouraged to gain hands-on experience in reputed corporate sector through internship projects while studying at the Department. Mini projects are promoted as part of their curriculum to supplement academic knowledge with practical experience. They also work on projects that benefit local industries or address real-time problems. These projects enable them to comprehend the value of working in a group as well as the finer aspects and significance of collaboration.


Our curriculum is intended to create a balance between professional knowledge and personal abilities. We are hopeful that our current curriculum will enable young executives to meet the corporate world's expectations in terms of overall development. We are dedicated in educating our students with not only technical knowledge but also leadership characteristics as an entrepreneur, allowing them to create jobs for others. This approach enables our students to work successfully in both multinational industries as well as an entrepreneur.
