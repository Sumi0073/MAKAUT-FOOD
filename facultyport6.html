Academic Qualification:

  • Ph.D. degree from Jadavpur University in 2011
  • Doctoral research from Indian Institute of Chemical Biology, Kolkata

Work Experience:

In 2017 Dr. Chandra Joined the Department of Biotechnology, MAKAUT, WB, as CSIR pool Scientist and served for teaching subjects like food Biotechnology, Food Microbiology etc. In 2019 she joined the Department of Food Technology at Techno India as Assistant Professor and then 2021 onwards she joined the Department of Food Science and Technology, MAKAUT, WB as assistant Professor

Research Areas:

During her doctoral work she gathered experience on radiation biology, radiation effect on food and biomaterials, nutraceuticals, radioactive tagging of edible and injectable protein and peptides and their metabolic effect. During her post-doctoral research at IICB she had worked on fat metabolism, obesity and clinical studies which was continued in her Senior Research Associateship at the department of Biotechnology, MAKAUT, WB. She has successfully completed projects through ICMR, CSIR fellowship She has published 13 research papers in various national and international journals. She has more than 20 research Proceedings. She worked as peer reviewer in many reputed journals. Currently Dr. Chandra is working on Food texturization, food analysis, innovative food development, nutraceuticals, metabolic effect, epidemiological study, survey work and clinical nutrition.

Outreach Activities:

Dr. Chandra is serving as a member of FSSAI- FOSTAC since 2016. She also has industrial exposure and consultancy experience in various food sectors like Bakery, Spice, Beverage etc. She is a life member of various scientific associations like Nutrition Society of India, AFSTI, Society of Biological Chemists.
